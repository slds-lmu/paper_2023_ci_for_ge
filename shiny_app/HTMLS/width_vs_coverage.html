Here, we see

  • x-axis: The average with of a CI method, relative to Bayle (10-fold CV, all-pairs estimator).
  • y-axis: The root-mean-square coverage error from the nominal level (0.95)
Both are calculated over all selected inducers or DGP, unless they are specified as the grouping variable.
The best methods are in the lower-left corner, i.e. they have low coverage error and a small width.
