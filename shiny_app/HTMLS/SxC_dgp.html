The plots show the coverage error (rmse) of a single selected DGP (y) for different dataset sizes (x).
This root-mean-square error to the nominal level is calculated over all inducers and inference methods, unless they are selected as either "Group" or "Color".
Methods that produce far too wide intervals (have an empirical coverage of around 100%) might be overlooked, see the "Width vs. Coverage Error" tab to get a better idea.
If there is only one size, the selected "Color" variable becomes the y axis.
